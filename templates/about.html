cowsource

Cowsource is a collaborative beef photo sharing platform: crowdsourcing cows.
Registered users can posts photos of their beef-containing food from a restaurant (currently only for Yelp restaurants).
Users can also discover food near them, search, or browse latest posts.
Developed by Sandy Fang, Katherine Gershfeld, and Dong Shin. Find us on Github
